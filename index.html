01
✦ Final Year Project · AIES 2025 Submission
💬
EMOSMS

A real-time, dual-model emotion detection system integrated into the open-source Signal messaging app. A binary RoBERTa classifier detects emotional presence; a multi-class model identifies the specific emotion — both using temporal context from the last 5 messages. Chat bubbles recolour dynamically to reflect emotional tone. Trained on DailyDialog, IEMOCAP & MELD. Evaluated with 15–20 participants: 80%+ reported improved emotional clarity in conversations.
